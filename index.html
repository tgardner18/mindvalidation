About The Owner

Kirby Gardner LCMHC

Starting therapy can feel both daunting and hopeful. I’m here to walk beside you with compassion, curiosity, and a solution-focused approach. Together, we’ll explore ways to support your emotional well-being and growth.

My work focuses on emotional regulation, anxiety, self-esteem, interpersonal relationships, and stress management. I’m licensed to practice in Massachusetts, New Hampshire, and Vermont, and bring over a decade of clinical experience to every session.

I hold a Master’s in Mental Health Counseling from Rivier University, where I first discovered my passion for evidence-based approaches to healing. I specialize in Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) and Dialectical Behavior Therapy (DBT) skills to help you build resilience and navigate life’s challenges—including anxiety, depression, and trauma-related concerns.
