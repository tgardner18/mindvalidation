About Me

Kirby Gardner, LCMHC

Licensed Mental Health Counselor with specialized training in CBT, mindfulness, and holistic wellness approaches

Professional Background

Kirby is a licensed mental health counselor with a specialization in mindfulness and Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT). She earned her certification in CBT from the prestigious Beck Institute in Pennsylvania, under the tutelage of Judith Beck. Her training was further enriched by an appearance from Aaron Beck, the esteemed founder of CBT.

Focus on Holistic Wellness

Kirby's practice is centered on holistic wellness, employing strategies to assist clients in confronting life's challenges through enhanced self-awareness and the encouragement of positive behavioral modifications.

Her empathetic methodology facilitates individuals in surmounting adversities, managing stress effectively, and improving their overall well-being.
