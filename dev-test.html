Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about therapy services, yoga classes, and how to get started.

What types of therapy do I offer?

Kirby Gardner, LCMHC offers Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT), Dialectical Behavior Therapy (DBT), trauma-focused therapy, and mindfulness-based approaches. All therapy is evidence-based and tailored to individual client needs, focusing on practical skills and lasting change.

Who can benefit from therapy at Mind Validation?

Mind Validation serves adults and adolescents ages 15 and up dealing with anxiety, depression, ADHD, trauma, emotional regulation challenges, relationship issues, stress management, and life transitions. Whether you're struggling with daily stress or working through deeper challenges, compassionate support is available.

Where is Mind Validation located?

Mind Validation is conveniently located in downtown Nashua, New Hampshire at 92 Main Street Suite #105. The entrance is between Casa Vieja Mexican Grill and Giant of Siam restaurants. The location is accessible through public transportation and street parking is typically available.

Does Kirby Gardner offer yoga classes?

Yes, Kirby is a Yoga Alliance certified instructor (RYT-200) offering mindful Vinyasa yoga classes by appointment. Classes are suitable for all levels and focus on mind-body connection, stress resilience, increased body awareness, and creating a gentle community experience with personalized guidance.

How do I schedule an appointment?

You can schedule a consultation by calling (951) 547-2909 or emailing mindvalidation@gmail.com. All therapy sessions and yoga classes are by appointment only. During your first consultation, we'll discuss your goals and determine the best approach for your wellness journey.

What makes Mind Validation different from other therapy practices?

Mind Validation combines clinical expertise with a holistic approach to wellness. Kirby's unique integration of evidence-based therapy (CBT, DBT) with mindfulness and yoga creates a comprehensive mind-body healing experience. With over a decade of clinical experience and multiple certifications, you receive compassionate, personalized care that addresses your whole self.
